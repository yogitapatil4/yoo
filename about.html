ABOUT US

Plants are amazing living organisms that belong to the kingdom Plantae. They are primarily characterized by their ability to perform photosynthesis, which is the process of converting light energy, usually from the sun, into chemical energy stored in sugars. This ability makes them fundamental to life on Earth, as they produce oxygen and serve as the base of most food chains.

Here are some interesting fact s about plants:

Diversity: There are over 390,000 known plant species, ranging from tiny mosses to giant sequoia trees.
Photosynthesis: This is the process by which plants convert sunlight into energy. It takes place in the chloroplasts within plant cells.
Reproduction: Plants reproduce in various ways, including seeds, spores, and even cloning (through vegetative propagation).
Medicinal Uses: Many plants have medicinal properties. For example, aspirin was originally derived from willow bark.
